How Our Rental System Works

RIDX offers a flexible and convenient car rental service. Whether you need a car for a few hours, days, or even weeks, we’ve got you covered. Here’s how it works:

  1. Choose your vehicle from our fleet above.
  2. Select your rental period (hourly, daily, or weekly).
  3. Complete the booking through our secure platform.
  4. Pick up your car from the nearest RIDX location or have it delivered.
  5. Enjoy your ride! Return the car at any RIDX location when done.
